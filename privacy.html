Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Dot is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Dot does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Data Storage

All data you enter in Dot (task names, dates, background images) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Dot does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Photos Access

Dot may request access to your photo library solely to let you choose a custom wallpaper background. Selected images are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Dot does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
